CROISSANT WORLD

La Fête du Croissant is, above all else, one big celebration! It’s fun, delicious and honors our group of artisans who make the best croissants you’ll ever taste. Once a year, a few weeks after Easter, more than 100 of the finest bakeries and pastry shops throughout Quebec come together and sell their hand-made croissants, freshly baked each morning. From Montreal to Îles-de-la-Madeleine, all participants agree that on this special day they will say thank you to their customers and offer each croissant for just $1.
